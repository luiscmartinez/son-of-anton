### Diff context

The change is intentionally small and tightly scoped:

1. `ACTIVITY_STATES` tuple grows from 13 entries to 15 by appending `"requesting_input"` and `"errored"`. No reordering. `activityStateSchema` is `z.enum(ACTIVITY_STATES)` and naturally picks up the new members.

   const schemaVersionField = z
     .number()
     .int()
     .min(1)
     .max(STATE_JSON_SCHEMA_VERSION);
